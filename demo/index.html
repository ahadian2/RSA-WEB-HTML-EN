Water Market

Rahrvan Sepehr Andisheh Company has been active in the field of water and energy management in the agricultural and industrial sectors for more than 15 years with the help of smart water and electricity meters. Installation of smart water meters begins in Tehran

The Tehran Provincial Water and Wastewater Company announced that the first phase of the project to install smart meters in the central areas of the city has begun. These meters are able to record instantaneous consumption and send the information online to the management system. According to officials, the use of smart meters can be up to 15 percent more effective in managing consumption and identifying leaks.

Learn more

Reducing Water Waste with Smart Meters

According to a report published today, the implementation of a pilot project to install smart meters in the city of Mashhad has reduced water waste by 12 percent in the past year. In addition to accurately recording consumption, these meters send alert messages to subscribers and allow them to pay bills based on actual consumption.

Learn more

Government supports domestic production of smart meters

The Ministry of Energy announced a program to support domestic companies in the production of smart water meters. The program aims to reduce dependence on imports and develop smart water resources management infrastructure. With the implementation of this plan, it is expected that more than two million smart meters will be installed in the country over the next three years.
