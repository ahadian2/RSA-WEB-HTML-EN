About Us

Rehrvan Sepehr Andisheh Knowledge-Based Company, a manufacturer of smart water and electricity meters in Iran, began its activities in 2002, simultaneously with the patent registration of the design and development of the idea of ​​measuring water consumption in a different and creative way. Since 2006, it officially entered the smart water and electricity meter market with the industrial production of a product that is distinct from existing meters; the smart water and electricity meter, as the only device that can meet the multi-year need of the country's water industry for measuring and managing groundwater aquifers, quickly established its position among managers and experts in the water and electricity field. Rehrvan Sepehr Andisheh Company has been able to create the world's largest water resources management project in Iran by supplying products and services to 30 provinces of the country.

This company, while assessing market needs at the national and global levels, relying on the high potential of its technical and engineering skills, has always relied on technical and quality improvement and strived to provide an innovative, reliable and efficient product for monitoring and managing groundwater resources. The company's solution has enabled employers to implement macro policies using a smart network not only in the water industry, but also in the field of energy consumption management. Rehrvan Sepehr Andisheh Company has started implementing projects for designing, manufacturing, installing, commissioning and operating smart water and electricity meters about two decades ago, and in this way, relying on the following influential factors, it has always been able to present itself as the best choice in the country's water and electricity industry in managing groundwater resources:

About Rehran Sepehr Andisheh

In 2002, a group of electronic engineers from Rehran Sepehr Andisheh Company, by conducting research on the idea of ​​a smart water meter, succeeded in testing and patenting the first smart meter model in the country and the world. In 2003, with the expansion of research and results, the next model of meters was designed, produced and tested in a more industrial way with success. Since 2004, the idea of ​​being able to supply and produce a new product in accordance with the required industrial standards has been on the agenda of this company.
