Smart meter model RK120

Since 2006, with the industrial production of a product that is distinct from existing meters, the smart water and electricity meter has officially entered the market; as the only device that can meet the multi-year needs of the country's water industry for measuring and managing groundwater aquifers, the smart water and electricity meter has quickly established its position among managers and experts in the water and electricity field. Rehrvan Sepehr Andisheh Company has been able to create the world's largest water resources management project in Iran by supplying products and services to 30 provinces of the country.

  • Compliant with the latest specifications of the National Smart Metering System
  • Ability to issue power outage orders at predetermined times for peak and demand management
  • Ability to cut off current up to 100A without using additional equipment (only in direct meters, depending on request)
  • Data transfer via optical port, replaceable internal modem, RS485 port and contactless smart card
  • Ability to read subordinate meters
  • Ability to use independent hardware encryption by security institutions
  • Ability to read during power outages via the screen and optical port (using replaceable batteries and Supercapacitor)

About Rehran Sepehr Andisheh

In 2002, a group of electronic engineers from Rehran Sepehr Andisheh Company, by conducting research on the idea of ​​a smart water meter, succeeded in testing and patenting the first smart meter model in the country and the world. In 2003, with the expansion of research and results, the next model of meters was designed, produced and tested in a more industrial way with success. Since 2004, the idea of ​​being able to supply and produce a new product in accordance with the required industrial standards has been on the agenda of this company.
