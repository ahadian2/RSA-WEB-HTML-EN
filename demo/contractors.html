Our Contractors

Our Contractors

Smart water meter contractors play an important role in modernizing and optimizing water supply networks. By installing and commissioning smart meters, these contractors enable real-time monitoring of water consumption, leak detection, and accurate billing for water and wastewater companies. In addition to implementing installation projects, they also provide maintenance, software support, and operator training. The use of smart meters increases measurement accuracy, reduces resource waste, and better manages water consumption, and contractors in this field play a key role in achieving the goals of smart water resource management.

About Rehran Sepehr Andisheh

In 2002, a group of electronic engineers from Rehran Sepehr Andisheh Company, by conducting research on the idea of ​​a smart water meter, succeeded in testing and patenting the first smart meter model in the country and the world. In 2003, with the expansion of research and results, the next model of meters was designed, produced and tested in a more industrial way with success. Since 2004, the idea of ​​being able to supply and produce a new product in accordance with the required industrial standards has been on the agenda of this company.
